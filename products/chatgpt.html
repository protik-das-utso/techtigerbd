What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an AI assistant that can understand instructions in natural language and generate helpful responses. It can draft and rewrite text, explain complex topics, create code snippets, summarize documents, analyze data, and even chat about PDFs and images when supported.

Why is it necessary?

  • Productivity: Save hours on emails, briefs, reports, and research summaries.
  • Coding help: Boilerplate, snippets, debugging ideas, and API examples in seconds.
  • Learning: Turn complex ideas into simple explanations with examples and step-by-step guides.
  • Creativity: Brainstorm campaign ideas, scripts, posts, and outlines on demand.
  • Multimodal: When enabled, reason over images/docs for faster understanding.
