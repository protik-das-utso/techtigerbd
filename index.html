Refund Policy

    • (c) Remaining Time: Number of unused subscription days from the time an issue is reported.
  • Clause 2 – Change of Mind
    • (a) Change-of-mind or "no longer needed" reasons are not eligible for refund once delivery is complete.
    • (b) All sales are final unless covered under service failure clauses below.
  • Clause 3 – Content or Language Specific Purchases

    • (a) If an account cannot be used, Tech Tiger BD will fix or replace it within 24 hours of your report.

    • (c) If you request a refund during the 24-hour cooldown, you will receive a 50% refund of the remaining time value.
  • Clause 7 – Non-Refundable Cases
    • (a) Change-of-mind, mistaken purchase, or lower-price found elsewhere.

    • (c) Problems caused by the user's internet, device, or VPN configuration.

    • (a) Submit requests via official Tech Tiger BD page or chat within the valid time frame.
